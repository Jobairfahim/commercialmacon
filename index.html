What We Do

Commercial Locksmith Services in Macon

Commercial buildings require reliable door hardware to operate safely and securely. We provide essential locksmith services for businesses across the Macon area.

🔑

Commercial Lock Rekey

Rekeying allows businesses to change which keys work without replacing the entire lock hardware. Recommended when employees leave the company or when keys are lost.

🚪

Panic Bar Repair & Installation

Panic bars are installed on commercial exit doors for quick emergency exits. Over time they may become loose or stop latching. We repair and install panic bar hardware for commercial doors.

🗝️

Master Key Systems

Master key systems allow managers to open multiple doors with one key while employees have access only to certain areas — simplifying access control for offices and commercial buildings.

🏢

Office Lockout Service

Office lockouts can happen when keys are misplaced or doors accidentally lock. Our mobile locksmith service restores access quickly while avoiding unnecessary damage.

🔧

Door Closer Repair

Door closers ensure commercial doors close smoothly and securely. When closers leak oil or lose adjustment, doors may slam shut or fail to close properly. We fix that.

Common Issues

Common Commercial Lock Problems

Businesses often contact a locksmith when they experience these issues. We handle all of them.

01
Broken Key in Lock

Keys can sometimes break inside the lock cylinder. A locksmith can safely remove the broken key and restore the lock.

02
Employee Left the Company

When employees leave, many businesses rekey their locks to restore control over building access.

03
Panic Bar Not Latching

Loose or worn panic bars may prevent a commercial door from closing and securing properly.

04
Door Closer Slamming

A door closer that has lost adjustment can cause doors to slam shut loudly and unsafely.

05
Key Stuck in Lock

If a key becomes stuck in the lock, forcing it may damage the cylinder. Let a professional handle it.

06
Lock Hard to Turn

Commercial locks may become difficult to turn due to wear or misalignment and need professional service.
